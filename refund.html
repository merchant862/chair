REFUNDS, CHARGEBACKS & BILLING DISPUTES

If you believe a charge was made in error, or if you have a billing question about a purchase made through DrawzOffers.com, please contact our support team before opening a bank dispute. We review refund and billing requests on a case-by-case basis and aim to respond within two (2) business days.

To help us locate your transaction quickly, include the name, email address, phone number, billing ZIP code, approximate purchase date, and a short description of the issue. Refund eligibility may depend on the transaction status, product access, support history, and applicable payment processor rules.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method whenever possible. Processing times vary by card issuer or financial institution, and it may take several business days for the credit to appear on your statement after approval.
